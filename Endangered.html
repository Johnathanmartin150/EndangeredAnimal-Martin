BORNEAN ORANGUTAN

What in the holy Animal Planet Channel is a Bornean Orangutan?

The Bornean Orangutan is a Giant orange ape that lives on island of the coast of Singapore and Indonesia. It is quite a big ape compared to others apes in that category.They stand from 3.3 feet to a max of 4.6 and just a little bigger than that with their weight making them look bigger cause they can weigh anywhere from 66 pounds to 220 pounds.

The Bornean Orangutan are have been in a drastic decline for more than 50% for the past 60 years according to studies making it a critically endangered animal.

Where did they swing from?

The main habitat of these curious creatures are in northern, southern, and central Borneo, where they are being watched and protected by some foundations such as the Borneo Orangutan Survival Foundation(BOS) have kept watched over them and helped them in times of need.
